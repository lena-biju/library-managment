Chapter 1: An Arrest

The investigation began on July 15th when a young boy's mutilated body was discovered in Flint City's park. Detective Ralph Anderson, having worked in law enforcement for over two decades, had never seen anything quite like it. The evidence was overwhelming, pointing to Terry Maitland, one of the city's most respected citizens.

But something wasn't adding up. How could a man be in two places at once?

As Ralph dug deeper into the case, he began to question everything he thought he knew about reality itself. The truth, he would soon discover, was far more terrifying than any crime he had investigated before.
